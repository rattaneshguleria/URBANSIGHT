Frequently Asked Questions

Does UrbanSight store personal data?

No. UrbanSight does not store personal identifiers. Videos are processed with face blurring and all data is deleted within 24 hours.

Can this system be used for mass surveillance?

UrbanSight is designed as an assistance tool for security operators, not a mass surveillance system. It requires human oversight for all actions and does not track individuals.

What about false alarms?

Our system uses threshold-based alerts that require human verification. We prioritize reducing false positives through contextual analysis and operator review.

Is UrbanSight replacing human security guards?

Absolutely not. UrbanSight is designed to assist human operators by filtering large volumes of footage and highlighting potential incidents, allowing guards to focus on critical situations.

Our Ethical Commitment

UrbanSight is built on the principle that technology should enhance security without compromising fundamental rights. We believe in surveillance that protects people, not surveillance that watches people.
